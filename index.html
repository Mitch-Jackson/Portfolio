About Me

I went to college at CSU Chico for health education. After I finished there I ended up continuing my education at UCLA Extension to become a licensed drug and alcohol counselor. For the next 8 years, I worked in treatment centers all over Los Angeles before getting certified in life coaching and starting my private practice.

One big thing I have taken away from all my experience is that I love helping people AND I love coding. Once I began my journey at Thinkful to learn software development I knew I had found my passion.

I would be honored to assist you on your next HTML, CSS, JavaScript, or React project.
